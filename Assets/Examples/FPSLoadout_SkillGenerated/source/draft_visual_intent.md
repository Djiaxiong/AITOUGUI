## Intent

- near-future tactical loadout screen
- compact control-room composition rather than marketing splash art
- strong panel hierarchy with bright weapon focus and restrained orange action accents

## Normalize Strategy

- preserve layout, hierarchy, and technical typography first
- express cut-corner intent through plate-like panels and outline framing
- do not rely on images, browser effects, or overflow tricks

## Hard Limits

- no fallback sprite assumptions
- no dashed borders
- no background images
- no browser-only filters or masks
