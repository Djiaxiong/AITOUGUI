## Intent

- tactical FPS HUD with calm, transparent overlays
- modules should read like real gameplay information, not poster composition
- center crosshair and edge HUD blocks must stay explicit and stable

## Normalize Strategy

- preserve anchor rectangles and information priority first
- keep the page procedural and image-free
- reduce decoration until the HUD remains directly importable

## Hard Limits

- no fixed-position browser tricks
- no images
- no dashed borders
- no layered composite templates
